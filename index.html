Support Resources

If your account has been compromised:

  • Change your password immediately: Update it on your service's website.
  • Revoke access: Log out from all devices if possible.
  • Check activity: Review your account for unauthorized actions.
  • Contact support: Reach out to the service provider.
  • Stay safe: Avoid suspicious links and downloads.

Account Security Tips

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Adding an extra layer of security by requiring a second form of verification to log in to your accounts.
  • Use a password manager: Helps you create and store unique passwords for each service, making it easier to avoid password reuse.
  • Beware of phishing attempts: Be cautious of emails, messages, or pop-ups that ask for your personal or financial information.
  • Regularly update your passwords: Change your passwords every few months and avoid using the same password across multiple sites.

Reporting Targeted Harassment

If you're experiencing targeted harassment or abuse online, it's important to take immediate action to protect yourself:

  • Document the harassment: Take screenshots, save messages, or keep records of any abusive content, as this can be used as evidence.
  • Report the harassment to the platform: Most social media and online platforms have mechanisms to report harassment. File a report with the relevant platform (e.g., Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, etc.) and follow their process to block or ban the harasser.
  • Contact your local authorities: If the harassment is severe or involves threats of violence, contact your local law enforcement. They can help investigate and may take legal action if necessary.
  • Reach out for support: Harassment can take a toll on mental health. Consider reaching out to friends, family, or a professional therapist for support. There are also online communities and hotlines dedicated to offering assistance for individuals facing harassment.
  • Stay anonymous when possible: If harassment is occurring on a platform, consider changing your privacy settings or using anonymous profiles to protect your personal information.
